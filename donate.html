Frequently Asked Questions

How is my donation used?

65% funds direct student programs (tuition, tools, mentorship), 20% develops the Kiver.org platform, 10% covers administrative costs, and 5% goes to marketing to reach more students.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! CoveOfEdu is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit (EIN: 92-2455469). You'll receive a tax receipt immediately after your donation.

Can I donate monthly?

Absolutely! Monthly donations help us plan sustainable student support. Contact us at donate@coveofedu.org to set up recurring donations.

Will I know which student I'm helping?

For donations of $2,500+, we can match you with a specific student (with their permission) and provide quarterly progress updates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards via Stripe. For wire transfers or stock donations over $10,000, please contact us directly.

Can my company match my donation?

Many employers offer donation matching! Check with your HR department. We'll provide all necessary documentation.
