What Makes This Different.

We're not building another after-school program. We're building the infrastructure that should have existed for every kid who had the fire but not the support. The kid who was searching, not failing — and got ignored because the system only knows how to catch you when you fall.

Plenty of organizations talk about purpose. Some have curricula. A few have technology. None of them have a card you scan that launches a teaching, guides an exercise, and then asks you at the end: did something just shift? And logs your answer in a counter that the whole world can see.
