Student Application Process

Ready to transform your life? Here's how to apply:

  1. Submit Initial Application: Fill out the form above or email apply@coveofedu.org
  2. Complete Questionnaire: You'll receive a detailed questionnaire about your goals and background
  3. Video Interview: 15-minute video call with our admissions team
  4. Donor Matching: We'll match you with a donor sponsor who believes in your potential
  5. Begin Transformation: Start your 90-day journey within 2 weeks

Note: Our program is fully funded by donors. There is no cost to accepted students. We prioritize students from underserved communities who demonstrate motivation and commitment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I hear back?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. Student applications may take 3-5 business days for initial review.

What if I'm not sure which category I fall into?

No problem! Just select "Other" in the contact form and describe your situation. We'll route your inquiry to the right team.

Can I schedule a call instead of sending a message?

Absolutely. Mention in your message that you'd prefer a call, and include your availability and time zone.

Do you accept international students?

Currently, our program serves students in the United States. However, we're planning international expansion. Contact us to join our waitlist.

How can I visit your offices?

CoveOfEdu is a fully remote organization. All our programs are delivered online, making them accessible to students nationwide.

Need Immediate Assistance?
