The Problem.

Counseling, career training, therapy… prepares people for a world that will not exist. Our world is exponentially changing. We need character development that transcends economic disruption.

The system catches the failing. It ignores the searching. Safety nets exist for kids falling behind—nothing exists for the kid reaching upward with empty hands.

I sit across from parents who can see brilliance in their children the way you can see a city from an airplane—clearly, completely, from a distance the child doesn't have. And those parents are desperate. Because they can see it, and their child cannot, and no amount of love or money or tutoring has been able to close that gap.

This is an epidemic that nobody talks about because these kids look extraordinary on paper. Schools can't reach them—these schools are often the source of the pressure. And therapy—look, therapy is wonderful. I'm a great believer in therapy. But therapy is a conversation about what's broken. These kids don't need to be fixed. They need to be shown.

Shown the thing inside them that everyone else can see but they can't. They need someone who sees the exception within them—the way my brother once saw it in me, when I was convinced there was nothing there worth finding.

Covenant of Education is the infrastructure for that gap.

Our Origin.

When I was a kid, I had fire inside me but no one to fan it. The system had programs for kids falling behind, but nothing for the kid reaching upward with empty hands.

Two experiences changed my life: a volunteer tutor who showed up three times a week, and a structured system that made my effort visible. Neither told me what to become. They created conditions where I could not help but become more of who I already was.

My brother did not have that. He took his own life. It took me a decade to process the grief and find my way to purpose. I started Covenant of Education so that the distance between lost and aimed would never be that far for anyone again.

We built the 7 Teachings—a philosophy and methodology that creates the conditions for self-discovery. We deliver it through physical NFC cards teenagers can scan for mentorship content, digital courses, facilitated group experiences, and eventually certified practitioners and institutional licensing.

Our moonshot is 100 million people discovering their purpose by 2040.

Right now we are proving the model works at small scale. We need partners who believe that purpose-finding should not be an accident of privilege. If you want to be part of building the infrastructure that catches people before they fall through the cracks, this is the moment.

Where Do You Fit?

The best way to make your dreams come true is by taking others along with you for the ride. This is not meant to be an inspirational quote. It's the principle by which we function.

Group A

You already show up for yourself every day, but you don't have the level of success you know you deserve. We show you how to show up for others in a way that gets you closer to your purpose—by mentoring and inspiring them towards success in your chosen direction.

Group B

You don't yet show up for yourself. In which case we are here for YOU. We will show up for you, until you are ready to show up for yourself—and can take others along with you on the journey to success.
