The Gap We Saw

In 2019, we noticed something disturbing. While wealthy students were building businesses, learning to code, and preparing for an AI-driven future, underserved students were stuck in the same outdated education system their parents endured.

The wealth gap wasn't just about money anymore.
It was about access to leverage.

Three Observations That Changed Everything

  • 01

    Rich students had ChatGPT accounts before most teachers knew what AI was.

  • 02

    Traditional education was teaching skills for jobs that would disappear in 5 years.

  • 03

    The students who needed transformation most had the least access to the tools that could change their lives.

How One Conversation Changed Everything

I'll never forget meeting one particular student. Brilliant. Driven. Full of potential. But when I asked what they were learning in school, I heard: Algebra II. The Revolutionary War. Shakespeare.

Nothing wrong with those subjects. But not a single thing about:
• How to build a business
• How AI will change the world
• How to create value
• How to find their purpose

That's when I knew we had to do something.
— The CoveOfEdu Founding Team

Beyond Skills: The 7 Teachings Movement

The technical skills were just the beginning. We discovered that students needed something deeper: A framework for living.

1

Self-Knowledge

Understanding who you are before deciding who you want to become.

2

Purpose Discovery

Finding the intersection of what you're good at and what the world needs.

3

Resilience Building

Developing the mental strength to persist through challenges.

4

Systems Thinking

Seeing the bigger picture and understanding how everything connects.

5

Value Creation

Learning to create real value in the world, not just collect paychecks.

6

Leadership

Leading yourself and others toward meaningful goals.

7

Legacy Thinking

Building something that lasts beyond your lifetime.

By 2023, we had created more than a curriculum.
We had created a philosophy for the next generation.
